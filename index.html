Research Interests:

My research interests primarily lie in Algorithms and Complexity Theory, focusing on Parameterized algorithm, Approximation, and Randomized. I am also interested Machine Learning. For more details, please view my CV.

Bio:

I am currently Postdoctoral Fellow at UCLA, working with Professors Eran Halperin and Sriram Sankararaman. I received my Ph.D. from University of Connecticut , working under the supervision of professor Mukul Bansal. My Ph.D. research focused on Algorithmic problems on computational biology, my Dissertation Title was Inferring Microbial Gene Family Evolution Using Duplication-Transfer-Loss Reconciliation: Algorithms and Complexity, you can find it here. I received my M.Sc. from Univesity of Tehran. My M.Sc. research focused on Randomized Approximation Algorithm, my Dissertation Title was Randomized Approximation Algorithm For Matroid Secretary Problem.
